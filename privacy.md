# Privacy Policy

  **Last updated: 11 May 2026**                                                                                                                                                      

  SkillSprout is designed for families. We don't sell data, don't show ads, and don't share information with third parties for marketing.

  ## What we collect
  - Child task data and parent settings are stored in the family's private iCloud (Apple's CloudKit). We do not host any backend that stores personal data.
  - Photos submitted by children as task proof are uploaded only to the family's iCloud and are stripped of EXIF/GPS/camera metadata before upload.
  - Children sign in via a parent-issued 6-digit code on their own device. No email, password, or PII is collected from children.

  ## What we don't do
  - No third-party analytics SDKs
  - No advertising SDKs
  - No data sold to third parties
  - No tracking across apps

  ## Account deletion
  You can delete your account from the parent profile inside the app. This cascades all data through CloudKit and is irreversible.
